Immerse yourself in the cuisine of the world most delicious cultures. Chef Missy will expand your experience with her own fabulous creations as well as offering a unique and enlivening take on familiar dishes. And, if you have the privilege to attend one of Chef Missy's exclusive pop-up diners, you will enjoy the pleasure of learning from her explorations throughout the world as well as in the kitchen.

Chef Missy

Missy graduated from the Auguste Escoffier School of Culinary Art, Colorado and embraced her love for food and flavor with an unfettered passion. Upon graduation she explored the culinary world gaining experience as a prep cook, line cook/garde manger, personal chef and caterer.

Missy's international travels, experience as a personal chef/caterer in addition to her industry experience have helped to mold her. She has a healthy respect, understanding and passion for food which has led to an intense pursuit of flavor, technique andpresentation.

Missy is a strong proponent of using the highest quality, local, farm-raised ingredients whenever possible.
