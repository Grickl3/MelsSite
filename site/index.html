Chef Missy

Missy graduated from the Auguste Escoffier School of Culinary Art, Colorado and embraced her love for food and flavor with an unfettered passion. Upon graduation she explored the culinary world gaining experience as a prep cook, line cook/garde manger, personal chef and caterer.

The combination of her international travels and industry experience shape her, culminating in a deep understanding of food & flavor. Missy believes the most flavorful cuisine is made with simple, quality ingredients and ballance. She considers it an honor to please your palate. Her understanding of flavor balance as well as her uniquely creative energy play a vital role the creation of her menus.
